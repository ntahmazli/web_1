About Student Life

Student Life is a website that collects all the fun aspects of ADA in one interactive platform. This is a passion project formed by third-year IT undergraduates to bring all of the extracurricular opportunities ADA offers to its students.

No more need to search for events in e-mails or social media! This website brings all the former and upcoming events held by these entities into one convenient location. You will find everything you need about any club or organization, from their goals and purpose to their chairman’s’ contact information.

As developers, we hope you enjoy using this platform as much as we enjoyed building it!
