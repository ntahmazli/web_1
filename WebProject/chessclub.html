Description

ADA CHESS CLUB is one of the clubs of ADA University. As we know, chess is a mind game and playing a chess helps the people who play it to increase his or her thinking style, improving the mind, helps to think logically and also helps to solve the problems more easily and all these characteristics are applied to the ADA University students under the leadership of ADA CHESS CLUB. The club has also collaborated with the ADA SCHOOL DIRECTORY to organize the chess lessons for 10th and 11th-grade students once a week. Apart from the lessons, the club organizes events 2 or 3 times during a semester. The club has recently collaborated with UNIVERSITY PRINCIPLE – HAFIZ PASAYEV to organize the big event of both students and high school children being participated and played with each other to gain the prices.
