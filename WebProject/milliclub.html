Description

Milli Club is established on the 15th of January 2018. Mission- Milli Club provides a new generation with information about customs and traditions. Our core values are to keep and raise awareness about our traditions and introduce them to the local and foreign students. Vision- Having started at ADA University, Milli Club aims to expand its horizon, attract more students and other stakeholders and become a unique brand in the whole Azerbaijan.
