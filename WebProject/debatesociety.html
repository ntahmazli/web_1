 Debate Soceity


Description

Debate Society is the community within ADA University that conducts debates for students to improve their critical thinking abilities and argumentation skills. To achieve a healthy environment for debates, we propose different motions that tackle global issues and world controversies. This club is run by Asnaya Zeynalli who is the president of the Debate Society and Vidadi Nasibov who is Vice-President. Lots of projects are being prepared by the DS team and hopefully will be implemented soon!
