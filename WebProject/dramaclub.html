Description

The main goal of our club is to help students who are interested in playing roles on the stage to spend their spare time in interesting activities. We provide theater programming to ADA students as a means of developing empathy, promoting academic growth and empowering the individual through self-expression.

We know that there are a lot of students who are interested in playing roles in different plays at our university. As, there is no other club to give them this opportunity, therefore we would like to enable them to improve their skills in this field.

We hope that due to our successful activities many students will join us and it will improve their abilities. Furthermore, we intend to reinforce student collaboration in cultural activities.
