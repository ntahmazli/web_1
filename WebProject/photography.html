Description

We are glad to present you with an unprecedented opportunity to evoke your creative visual side, to portray your passion, your vigor, your strengths and your weaknesses, your love on a piece of paper. We, ADA Photography Club - the first-ever avant-garde club in our university that specializes in photography, are going to provide students with everything they need to work on and improve their artistic skills. We are going to organize seminars, workshops on the subtleties of photography, work-exhibitions of members of our club, popular photographers, photoshoots of various events, and many more.
