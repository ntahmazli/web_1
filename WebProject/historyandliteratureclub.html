Description

The purpose of the History & Literature Club is to promote awareness about important historical events, as well as the importance of different individuals and communities to reach where we are on the current day. Besides that, we contribute to the discussion of famous classical and modern stories and the intricate messages behind them. We as History and Literature Club aim to spread the History and Literature spirit among ADA students to increase the quality of knowledge through having fun and getting awards.
