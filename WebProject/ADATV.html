Description

ADA TV is a pioneering project which was launched in Fall 2019. ADA TV aims to serve as a media platform for ADA Community where students, teachers, and staff can voice their opinions and creatively express themselves. ADA TV team consists of Videographers, Presenters/Journalists, Scriptwriters, and the Administration. The videos for the projects are being streamed on social media platforms, including Facebook, YouTube, and Instagram, which ensures unobstructed access to information. Our projects within ADA TV include monthly ADA NEWS videos, which aim to inform our audience about the events taking place on ADA Campus, as well as the "One minute" exchange series by former outgoing exchange students and "25 questions" series with staff members. More projects are soon to come, so #StayTuned!
