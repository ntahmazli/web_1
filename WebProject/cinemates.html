Description

The CineMates club was created to show people classical movies in the renaissance of enthusiasts who made movies as part of our culture. We not only focused on showing movies but also explaining them. So, before each event, we are shoving brief information about the director’s vision on a given movie and doing discussions on them. Also, we arrange documentary movie events where directors presenting their films only for our ADA students. Moreover, we have two constant charity events:
DoNation: all money from event’s tickets, are sent to needed ones (sick children, orphanages).
CartoonTime: CineMates members in collaboration with other clubs or students, visiting hospitals and orphanages to spend time with children watching cartoons with popcorn and fruits.
Our club also provides an opportunity to become acquainted with classical Azerbaijan movies. Additionally, we are writing English subtitles by our self for Azerbaijan classical movies to give international students learn more about our culture. To add more colors to holidays we are presenting them traditional movies which are representing a given holiday. We aim to gather people together where they can watch and discuss foreign and local classical movies and more importantly to enjoy them.
