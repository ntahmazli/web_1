Description

Art & Creativity Club gathers and promotes young, talented, and creative individuals for enriching their opportunities as artists in each of the aspects of art. From art and music to literature, poetry, and fashion, such events as exhibitions, competitions, weekly Academic Art classes, and masterclasses are perfect gateways to connect with the art community. Creativity is what assembles young people who are prepared for the rapidly changing work and living environment in the 21st century, turning new and imaginative ideas into reality. Combined with art, creativity is indispensable for the growth and sustainable development of societies and of individuals. The goals of our club are; Identification and promotion of talents, development of creative potential, and uniting creative youth of ADA University and ADA School. Making life more creative! Join and be part of the ADA Art & Creativity Club!
