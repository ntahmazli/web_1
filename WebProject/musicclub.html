Description

Music Club, like any other student club, enriches the student experience, encourages involvement and provides opportunities for students to express themselves. Music Club of ADA University’s main goal is to identify and encourage the Music Talent among the students and, meanwhile, encourage the members of the club to participate in a wide range of musical events conducted within a university in the frames of cultural activities. Music Club also strives to reveal the talent in each of the members by creating an environment for mutual learning. It includes singing, playing a variety of musical instruments and on top of that, exploring improvisation. We also cooperate with other student clubs and bring together those genuinely enjoying music and able to express it in their own unique way.
