Description

ADA Book Club is one of the oldest clubs at ADA University. The Book Club organizes various events, such as interesting games, book presentations, and movie days to improve the reading and critical thinking skills of the students. The Book Club activities contribute to the student life of the members interactively while expanding their knowledge and worldview. Once a week, the Book Club Members gather and engage in discussing and sharing their own opinions about different books, authors, and writing styles that they have read and analyzed. In the events of the club, the members get new acquaintances who have common interests and mindset with them. The primary purpose of our club is to encourage more students to read numerous books as well as to make them more enthusiastic about literature.
