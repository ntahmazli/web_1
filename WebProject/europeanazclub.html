Description

European Azerbaijan Club (EA Club) is primarily organized for providing students with knowledge and real experience of EU and EU-Azerbaijani relations. The Club also aims to play a key role in creating opportunities for students wishing to develop a career in the related fields. It was established in 2018 September, and since then, we had several events such as EU Brain Challenge Competitions, EU Lecture Series and Model of European Council 2018, Model EU Council 2019. Our main objective is to create a platform for the youth where they can develop their critical thinking, public speaking, and political skills, broaden their knowledge about European Union affairs and Azerbaijan-EU relations, exchange perspectives and viewpoints with different people, and improve their network. Therefore, our main activities include, but not limited to, conducting trainings and lectures, running historical simulations, and organizing conferences. President of European Azerbaijan Club is Rahila Aghababayeva who is Public Affairs 2020 student. The club is comprised of 4 main departments, External Affairs and Fundraising Department, Logistics Department, Public Relations Department and Academic Department. Each of them is assigned to a department head, members and functions.
